17 product categories

Everything your production needs.

From PP spunbond and cotton fabrics to latex, motors, and steel wire: the complete range of materials for bed and soft furniture manufacturing.

Product catalogue

To enquire about any product, contact our team.

01

PP Spunbond

Nonwoven polypropylene fabric used as backing cloth, insulator layer, and dust cover in mattresses, divan bases, and bed frames. Available in multiple gramme weights from lightweight lining to reinforcement grades.

Enquire →
02

Polyester Fiber

Loose and batted polyester filling for pillows, cushion inserts, and mattress comfort layers. Available siliconized and non-siliconized, in various denier counts and gram weights for different firmness profiles.

Enquire →
03

Motors & Remote Controls

Electric adjustment motors, lifting columns, and wired/wireless remote control systems for motorised and adjustable beds. Compatible with standard bed frame profiles. Single and dual motor configurations available.

Enquire →
04

Cotton Fabric

Natural woven cotton for mattress ticking, pillow covers, and quilted bed covers. Available in plain weave, jacquard, and printed constructions. Breathable natural fibre suited to premium bed products.

Enquire →
05

Latex

Natural and synthetic latex foam for mattress cores, toppers, and pillow inserts. Multiple ILD ratings available for varying comfort profiles from soft to extra firm. Dunlop and Talalay processing grades.

Enquire →
06

Polyester Wadding

Roll and cut wadding for quilted mattress covers, pillow shells, and top mattress comfort layers. Various gram weights; supplied with or without thermal bonding treatment for quilt stitch retention.

Enquire →
07

Zippers & Sliders

Nylon and metal zippers for mattress covers, pillow covers, and removable bed components. Supplied as continuous chain cut to length, or as pre-cut lengths complete with sliders. Self-locking slider options available.

Enquire →
08

Metal Connectors

Joining brackets, corner plates, slat holders, and frame connectors for bed construction. Available in multiple steel gauges and surface finishes for box spring, slatted base, and continental bed frame assembly.

Enquire →
09

Steel Wire

Bonnell and pocket coil spring units, perimeter border wire, and straightened steel wire for mattress core construction. Consistent gauge and temper for reliable long-term spring performance.

Enquire →
10

Leather

Genuine and PU synthetic leather for bed headboards, side rails, and decorative panels. Full range of surface finishes and colours. Supplied in rolls or cut to panel dimensions to your specification.

Enquire →
11

Cardboard Rolls

Perimeter edge rolls and reinforcement board for mattress side stiffening and box spring frame construction. Multiple thicknesses and widths. Kraft and recycled board grades to match specification requirements.

Enquire →
12

Duck Feathers

Down and feather blend filling for premium pillows, comforters, and luxury mattress toppers. Cleaned and processed, supplied by fill weight. Various down-to-feather ratios for softness and loft requirements.

Enquire →
13

Legs

Wooden and metal legs for divan bases, platform beds, and bed frames. Standard and custom heights. Natural wood, lacquered, and powder-coated metal options. Matching levelling glides and floor caps available.

Enquire →
14

Fibertex

Technical nonwoven fabric for mattress insulator pads, dust covers, and protective lining in bed base construction. Superior load distribution compared to standard PP spunbond; suited to high-specification mattresses.

Enquire →
15

Laminated Foam

Pre-laminated foam composite panels for headboards, quilted cover inserts, and comfort layers requiring pre-bonded foam-and-fabric construction. Custom foam grades and cover fabric combinations available on order.

Enquire →
16

Polyethylene Foil Rolls

PE film rolls for mattress and pillow packaging, moisture protection during transit, and wrapping of finished bed components. Available in standard roll widths and film gauges for manual and machine application.

Enquire →
17

Plastic Washers

Nylon and plastic washers, leg cups, and floor glides for bed frame assembly. Prevents floor damage and reduces movement. Supplied in standard packs to match common leg diameters and profiles.

Enquire →
