From the first fabric swatch to the final packaging — TAD stocks the complete range of materials for soft furniture manufacturing.

Product catalogue

To enquire about any product, contact our team.

01

Upholstery Fabrics

Wide-format rolls in pattern and solid weaves. Our range covers performance textiles, natural blends, and synthetic options for every seat application. Available in continuous lengths with consistent dye lots.

Enquire →
02

Velvet & Chenille

Cut pile and loop pile specialty textiles for premium upholstery. Includes plain velvets, structured chenilles, and embossed varieties suitable for both seats and decorative cushions.

Enquire →
03

Technical Fabrics

Backing cloths, dust covers, non-woven materials, and lining fabrics. The functional textiles that don't show but matter to quality and durability — fire-retardant grades available.

Enquire →
04

Polyurethane Foam

Seat, back, and cushion foam in all standard densities and grades. Sheet and profile cuts available. HR foam, memory foam, and reconstituted chipfoam for every comfort and budget requirement.

Enquire →
05

Hollowfiber & Padding

Filling fibers, batting, and pillow padding materials. Siliconized and non-siliconized hollowfiber, carded fiber batting in varying weights, and blended polyester wadding for loose cushions.

Enquire →
06

Springs & Sinuous Wire

Coil springs, zigzag sinuous wire, border wire, and spring clips for traditional and modern seat construction. Consistent gauge and temper for reliable performance throughout the product lifetime.

Enquire →
07

Mechanisms & Recliners

Sofa bed pull-out mechanisms, recliner chair actions, corner unit fittings, and storage bed hardware. Tested to European load and cycle standards.

Enquire →
08

Legs & Feet

Wooden, metal, and plastic furniture legs in standard and custom heights. Turned wood, tapered, hairpin, and block styles. Protective glides and leveling feet in matching finishes.

Enquire →
10

Webbing & Strapping

Elastic and non-elastic seat webbing, flat jute webbing, and polypropylene strapping. The foundation of every upholstered seat — stocked in standard widths and stretch percentages.

Enquire →
17

Metal Hardware & Fittings

Joining plates, corner braces, cam locks, barrel nuts, and assembly hardware for flat-pack and frame construction. Staples, tacks, and upholstery nails in all standard sizes.

Enquire →
09

Zippers & Closures

Nylon and metal zippers for cushion covers, bolster covers, and slipcovers. Continuous zipper chain cut to length, or pre-cut lengths with sliders. Self-sealing and locking slider options.

Enquire →
11

Decorative Trim & Piping

Welt cord, insertion piping, fringe, gimp, and decorative braid. Fabric-covered piping in standard colors, or piping cord for custom fabric covers. Fringe and tassel trims for traditional styles.

Enquire →
12

Buttons & Studs

Upholstery buttons in fabric-covered and self-covered styles, decorative nail studs in brass and nickel finishes, and tufting buttons with pull-through cord. Standard sizes and custom mold options.

Enquire →
13

Thread & Needles

Industrial sewing thread in polyester, cotton-wrapped polyester, and bonded nylon. Cone sizes for industrial machines. Upholstery needles, curved needles, and mattress needles in full size ranges.

Enquire →
14

Labels & Marking

Care labels, brand labels, and regulatory compliance labels. Woven and printed options. Blank swing tags and barcode labels. Custom print runs available on minimum order quantities.

Enquire →
15

Packaging & Protection

Corner protectors, edge guards, stretch film, polythene bags, corrugated sheet, and strapping. All protective packaging formats needed for finished furniture dispatch — single-piece to full-pallet quantities.

Enquire →
16

Adhesives & Chemicals

Contact adhesives, hot melt glue sticks, spray adhesives, and fabric glue for upholstery bonding. Water-based and solvent-based formulations. Fabric protector sprays and leather conditioners.
