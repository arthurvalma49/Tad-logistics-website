# TAD Logistics — Product Context

## Product Purpose
Corporate marketing website for TAD Logistics (Trading · Assembling · Distribution), an Estonian B2B supplier of materials and components to soft furniture manufacturers. The site communicates capability, range, and reliability to procurement contacts and manufacturing partners.

## Users
B2B buyers and procurement managers at soft furniture manufacturers in Estonia and the Baltics. They visit to verify that TAD stocks what they need, understand the service offering, and find a contact. They are practical, time-pressured, and trust is earned through clarity and professionalism — not marketing flair.

## Brand
- **Name:** TAD Logistics
- **Founded:** 2005
- **Tagline:** Trading · Assembling · Distribution
- **Tone:** Professional, understated, reliable. No hyperbole. No consumer marketing language.
- **Anti-references:** Discount/commodity distributors, overly flashy logistics tech startups, generic Fiverr B2B templates

## Services
1. **Trading** — 17 product categories: fabrics, fibers, foam, hardware, and accessories for soft furniture production
2. **Production** — Soft furniture manufacturing on a project basis
3. **Assembly** — Component assembly services

## Design Direction
Direction C — Clean Scandinavian Catalogue. Approachable, organized, trustworthy. Easy to browse. Feels like a well-designed product catalogue.

## Strategic Principles
- Clarity over cleverness
- Show range (17 categories) prominently
- Trust signals: year established, partner brands, team names, locations
- No hard sells — buyers will make contact when ready
